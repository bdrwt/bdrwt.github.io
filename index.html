About the Trust

The Baggavalli Doddasomappa Rural Welfare Trust is a community-driven initiative committed to transforming the lives of rural youth through the power of education and well-being. Named in honour of a respected elder whose life was rooted in service and simplicity, the Trust draws inspiration from his values of compassion, integrity, and dedication to the upliftment of society.

At the heart of the Trust’s mission is the improvement of quality education in the local government high school. The Trust is actively working to upgrade the infrastructure of the school, ensuring that classrooms are safe, well-equipped, and conducive to learning. From modern classrooms to science labs and libraries, the focus is on creating an environment where students can dream big and learn with dignity.

Our Mission in Action

Paving the way for a healthier, better-educated, and more empowered rural future.

Upgrading School Infrastructure

The Trust is actively working to upgrade the infrastructure of the local government high school, ensuring that classrooms are safe, well-equipped, and conducive to learning. From modern classrooms to science labs and libraries, the focus is on creating an environment where students can dream big and learn with dignity.

Promoting Experiential Learning

Recognizing that learning must go beyond textbooks, the Trust is introducing experiential learning methods—including hands-on science experiments, project-based learning, skill development workshops, and digital literacy initiatives—to spark curiosity and prepare students for real-world challenges.

Scholarships and Financial Aid

To support meritorious and economically disadvantaged students, the Trust offers scholarships and financial aid, ensuring that no child is forced to drop out due to lack of resources.

Health and Hygiene Initiatives

Beyond education, the Trust also works to improve the health and hygiene standards in the village, especially among schoolchildren. It supports initiatives related to clean drinking water, sanitation facilities, health camps, and awareness drives on nutrition and hygiene.

Community Engagement

Through sustained community engagement and a strong sense of purpose, the Baggavalli Doddasomappa Rural Welfare Trust is paving the way for a healthier, better-educated, and more empowered rural future.
